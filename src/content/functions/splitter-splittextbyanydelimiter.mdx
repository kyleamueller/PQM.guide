## Remarks

`Splitter.SplitTextByAnyDelimiter` returns a **function** that splits text at every occurrence of any delimiter in the provided list. All delimiters are treated equivalently â€” the text is split wherever any of them appear. This is useful when your data may use inconsistent separators (e.g., commas, semicolons, or pipes).

## Examples

### Example 1: Split on multiple possible delimiters

Split employee phone numbers that may use dashes or dots as separators.

Input: `Employees`

```powerquery
let
    SplitFn = Splitter.SplitTextByAnyDelimiter({"-", "."}),
    PhoneParts = SplitFn("555-867-5309")
in
    #table({"Part1", "Part2", "Part3"}, {{PhoneParts{0}, PhoneParts{1}, PhoneParts{2}}})
```
