## Remarks

`Text.Insert` inserts `newText` into `text` at the zero-based `offset` position. All characters at and after `offset` in the original text are shifted right to accommodate the inserted content.

If `text` is `null`, the function returns `null`. An `offset` of 0 prepends `newText` to the beginning of `text`. An `offset` equal to the length of `text` appends `newText` to the end.

This is equivalent to: `Text.Start(text, offset) & newText & Text.End(text, Text.Length(text) - offset)`

## Examples

### Example 1: Insert a space between two words

```powerquery
Text.Insert("HelloWorld", 5, " ")
```
